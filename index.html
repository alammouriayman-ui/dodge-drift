Dodge Drift — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This privacy policy explains how Dodge Drift ("the game", "we", "us") handles information when you play.

Who operates this game

Dodge Drift is developed by Ayman, an independent developer.

Information collected

Dodge Drift uses Epic Online Services (EOS) and Epic Account Services (EAS) to provide sign-in, achievements, and related features. When you sign in with your Epic Games account, Epic Games processes your account information according to its own privacy policy: epicgames.com/site/privacypolicy

Dodge Drift itself does not independently collect, store, or sell any personal information beyond what is necessary to operate these Epic-provided features, such as:

  • Your Epic Account ID (used to identify you for sign-in and achievements)
  • Gameplay statistics relevant to achievements (e.g., high scores)

Information we do not collect

Dodge Drift does not collect your real name, email address, payment information, or location data directly. Any such data, if collected, is handled exclusively through Epic Games' own systems under their privacy policy.

Children's privacy

Dodge Drift is not intended for use by children without appropriate parental consent mechanisms provided by the platform (e.g., Epic Games' Kids Web Services where applicable). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what Epic Games' platform-level protections already require.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share player data with third parties for advertising purposes. Any data shared with Epic Games is solely for the purpose of providing sign-in, achievements, and related platform services.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, you can reach the developer at: la1ry@yahoo.com

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the game evolves. Continued use of Dodge Drift after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
