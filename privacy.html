Dodge Drift

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

This privacy policy explains how Dodge Drift handles information when you play the game.

Who Operates This Game

Dodge Drift is developed by Ayman, an independent developer.

Information Collected

Dodge Drift uses Epic Online Services and Epic Account Services for sign-in, achievements, and related platform features. Epic Games processes Epic account information under its own privacy policy.

Dodge Drift does not independently collect, store, or sell personal information beyond what is needed to operate Epic-provided features, such as an Epic Account ID for sign-in and gameplay statistics used for achievements.

Information We Do Not Collect

Dodge Drift does not directly collect real names, email addresses, payment information, or location data. If those details are processed, they are handled by Epic Games systems.

Children's Privacy

Dodge Drift is not intended for use by children without appropriate platform-level parental consent mechanisms. The game does not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what Epic Games platform protections require.

Data Sharing

Dodge Drift does not sell or share player data with third parties for advertising purposes. Any data shared with Epic Games is used only to provide sign-in, achievements, and related platform services.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer at la1ry@yahoo.com.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as Dodge Drift evolves. Continued use of the game after changes means acceptance of the revised policy.
